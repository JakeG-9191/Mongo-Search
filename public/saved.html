Welcome to Your Saved Articles!


  1. Click an article below to bring up additionals options.

  2. First, similar to the Home Page, you have the option to visit the article in question.

  3. You may also choose to remove this article from your saved articles section.

  4. By adding a title and text to the next section, you can save a comment on the article, then click on "Save Your Comment" to add the comment to the associated article. This comment will be displayed in the box below the next time you click on the article in the left section. Only one comment per article can be saved right now, new comments will overwrite previous iterations.

  5. Finally, you can delete saved comments on articles, this will immediatly clear the comment, and if you click on the article again in the left section that article will be gone.
